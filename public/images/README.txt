HOMEPAGE HERO IMAGE
===================
Drop your hero image directly in this folder:

  hero.jpg   ← used by the homepage hero section

Recommended size: 1200 × 900 px, JPEG or WebP

SUBFOLDERS
----------
  team/          → Team member headshots
  events/        → Event thumbnail images
  events/photos/ → Event photo gallery images
  testimonials/  → Optional testimonial author photos
  logos/         → Sponsor / partner logos
